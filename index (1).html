The Errandeck Promise
🛡️

Your satisfaction,
guaranteed.

If your errand isn't completed to your satisfaction for any reason, Errandeck will work to make it right — full stop.

What's covered

✓

Task not completed

Tasker accepts but doesn't follow through? Full refund, no questions asked.

✓

Wrong or damaged items

Items bought are incorrect or damaged during delivery? We'll resolve it immediately.

✓

Late or no-show Tasker

Tasker doesn't show up or is significantly late without notice? You pay nothing.

✓

Payment dispute

Your money is held in escrow until YOU confirm the task is done. We never release it early.

How escrow protects you

1

You pay — funds locked securely in escrow

2

Tasker completes your errand with live updates

3

You confirm → payment released. Simple.

Have an issue?

Tap "Raise dispute" on any task within 24 hours of completion. Our support team responds within 2 hours, 7 days a week.
